Learn How to Make Tomato Sauce

Making tomato sauce from scratch is a great way to enhance the flavor of your pasta dishes. Follow our simple recipe to create delicious homemade tomato sauce in no time.

Get Started

How to Make Tomato Sauce

  1. Heat olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onions and garlic and cook until they are soft and translucent, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes, salt, pepper, and any other seasonings you prefer, such as oregano, basil, or red pepper flakes.
  4. Reduce heat to low and let the sauce simmer for about 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Remove the sauce from heat and let it cool slightly.
  6. Use an immersion blender or transfer the sauce to a blender and puree until smooth.
  7. Return the sauce to the saucepan and simmer for an additional 5-10 minutes.
  8. Adjust seasoning to taste and serve.

Enjoy your homemade tomato sauce!

Ingredients you will need:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 28 oz canned tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: oregano, basil, red pepper flakes
